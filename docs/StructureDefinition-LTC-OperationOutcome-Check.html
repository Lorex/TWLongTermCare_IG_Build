Resource Profile: 長照 SDK－跨月檢核結果（QP_O）

Official URL: http://ltc-ig.fhir.tw/StructureDefinition/LTC-OperationOutcome-Check Version: 1.0.0
Draft as of 2026-04-02 Computable Name: LTCOperationOutcomeCheck
